Miss Katie started her training with Vanda Morgan, with whom she stayed until the age of 18 before flying the nest to study law and child psychology at the University of Warwick. Although fully equipped for a career in the big city, she knew her true passion was dancing and that nothing else could compete! She went on to train professionally at the Masters Performing Arts College and gain her teaching qualifications with the ISTD and IDTA.

Miss Katie has since taught many many children and students, choreographed for numerous successful shows, festivals and competitions, as well as entering children into exams with a 100% pass rate with the ISTD and IDTA.
