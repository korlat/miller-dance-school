Miss Katie started her training with Vanda Morgan completing her ISTD and RAD exams until the age of 18 before flying the nest to study law and child psychology at the University of Warwick.

Although fully equipped for a career in the big city, she knew her true passion was dancing and that nothing else could compete! She went on to train professionally at the Masters Performing Arts College and more recently with the Royal Academy of Dance.

Miss Katie has since taught many many children and students, choreographed for numerous successful shows, festivals and competitions, as well as entering children into exams with a 100% pass rate.

Suzannah Botziou

LRAD, ARAD (Adv) AISTD (Modern, National), Associate Ballroom

Miss Suzie has been teaching ballet for over 30 years. She began her early training at the age of 7 with Miss Catherine Blellock and later trained at Bush Davies full time school in East Grinstead. She went on to study at the Royal Academy of Dancing gaining all of her teaching qualifications and was then offered a dancing post with the NAFSICA Ballet and Contemporary Company in Athens. She has worked as a freelance ballet teacher for various schools including the Italia Conti school in London.

She has three grown up children and two grandchildren. She is looking forward to seeing the show and wishes all the students lots of luck.

Max Alan

Max came to Miller School of Dance in the summer and has been with us since then teaching Musical Theatre and Commercial.
