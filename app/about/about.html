Although she always enjoyed performing and being in front of the curtain, her passion and true talent is in teaching and choreography. Miss Katie has worked as lead choreographer for many schools and production companies and often guest choreographs on various projects. Miss Katie started her training with Vanda Morgan completing her ISTD and RAD exams at the age of 18 before flying the nest to study law and child psychology at the University of Warwick.

Although fully equipped for a career in the big city, she knew her true passion was dancing and that nothing else could compete! She went on to train professionally at the Masters Performing Arts College and more recently with the Royal Academy of Dance.

Miss Katie has since taught many many children and students, choreographed for numerous successful shows, festivals and competitions, as well as entering children into exams with a 100% pass rate.
