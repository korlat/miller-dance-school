Dance school in Southend-on-Sea

Classes are held in RAD Ballet, ISTD Tap, Modern and Jazz. Examinations are entered for throughout the year as well as Festivals and Choreographic Competitions.

The schools aim is to encourage and achieve a high standard of dance;

inspiring students of all ages to reach their full potential and giving them opportunity to experience the enjoyment of participating in theatre and performance.
